Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email address, and Profile picture (via Google Sign-In).
  • Health Data: Steps, Sleep, Height, Weight, and other vitals (synced via Google Fit or HealthKit with your explicit permission).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, such as feature usage and crash logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To sync your data across devices securely.
  • To personalize your experience and provide health insights.
  • To communicate with you about updates and support.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate our app:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and analytics.
  • Sentry: For error tracking and performance monitoring.
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions and purchases.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this directly within the app by navigating to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Upon deletion, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
